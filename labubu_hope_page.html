Born from the imagination of Hong Kong artist Kasing Lung and produced by POP MART, Labubu has become one of the most beloved art toy characters in the world. With its pointy ears, mischievous grin, and timeless childlike energy, Labubu stands as a symbol of creativity, innocence, and joy.

Each edition tells a different story — from playful adventures to moments of quiet reflection. The series spans seasonal lines, artist collaborations, and special event releases, all united by a singular design language that is instantly recognizable yet endlessly expressive.

A Character with a Story

First appearing in Kasing Lung's illustrated fantasy world The Monsters, Labubu was imagined as a curious and slightly mischievous creature — sometimes bold, sometimes shy, always authentic. Through themed series like Forest Labubu, Starry Labubu, and Wizard Labubu, the character evolved into a multi‑dimensional symbol of emotion and imagination.

At its heart, Labubu celebrates the imperfect joy of childhood. The exaggerated grin and bright, curious eyes invite us to embrace oddness, sincerity, and wonder — a small reminder that feeling deeply is a form of courage.

Series Guide — Themes & Notable Editions

Design Language

Pointy ears, a wide mischievous smile, and a compact silhouette define Labubu's core. Editions vary finishes (matte/gloss), palettes (pastel to vivid), and accessories (hats, capes, critters) while preserving an iconic shape.

Notable Lines

  • Seasonal series (Spring/Rainy Day/Sleepy)
  • Fantasy series (Wizard/Starry/Forest)
  • Event & collaboration editions
  • Blind‑box surprises and chases

Materials & Finish

Durable PVC/ABS typical of designer toys; select runs feature flocking, translucent parts, or glitter finishes. Hand‑finished paint apps add subtle variation — each piece carries small nuances.

Care & Display

Avoid direct sun and high humidity. Dust with a soft brush. For display, diffused light highlights sculpted contours; risers or mirror backs can enhance shelf presence.

Collector's Guide — Buying & Authenticity

  • Boxes & COA: Keep original packaging and any certificates; they help maintain long‑term value.
  • Release Info: Verify edition names, release windows, and paint variants before purchasing.
  • Condition: Inspect for paint transfers or factory marks; minor variations are common in hand‑finished toys.
  • Community: Join collector forums/groups for trade references and photo comparisons.

FAQ

What is Labubu "Hope" in the series lineup?

"Hope" is a thematic edition that emphasizes warmth and optimism through a softer palette and calm facial expression. It represents the reflective, comforting side of Labubu while retaining the character's iconic silhouette.

What are typical sizes and materials?

Most Labubu figures are made from PVC/ABS and commonly range from ~8–12cm (blind-box) to larger display pieces depending on the release. Some runs feature flocking, translucent parts, or glitter finishes.

How can I verify authenticity?

Buy from official POP MART channels or trusted retailers, keep original boxes and any certificates, and compare paint apps and packaging details with official references. Community groups can help with side-by-side photo checks.

How should I care for and display figures?

Avoid direct sunlight and high humidity. Use a soft, dry brush for dusting. For flocked parts, do not use liquids; for standard PVC/ABS, a microfiber cloth works well. Diffused lighting and risers help showcase sculpted contours.
